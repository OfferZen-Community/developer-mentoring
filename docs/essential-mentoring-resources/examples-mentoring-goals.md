> ### 3. ‚ÄúSihle is a mentee. She‚Äôs just started her new job as a backend developer at a national e-commerce company. Sihle is relatively new to the industry. This is a multifaceted situation. There are _many_ reasons for why Sihle might be panicking when facing a new project. As Sihle‚Äôs mentor, what approach would you use? 

A deep-dive discussion might be helpful here, with a special focus on any previous occasions where Sihle might have panicked when facing a new project at work. > ### 4. ‚ÄúDimitri is a mentor and has come to a point in his career where he could either become super specialised or take on a more managerial position.
